Smart Mates are the fastest way to add mates — drag a part close to where it should go, and SolidWorks detects the correct mate type automatically when edges/faces align.
Always Fix the first part in an assembly (right-click → Fix) to anchor it to the origin. All other parts are then mated relative to this fixed component.

SolidWorks drawings are fully associative — when you change the 3D model, all dimensions and views in the drawing update automatically. This is a major workflow advantage over manual drafting.
Use eDrawings to share your designs with clients and manufacturers who don't have SolidWorks — they can view, rotate, and measure the model for free.
