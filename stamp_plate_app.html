🔄 Complete Workflow: AI Image → Stamp Plate SVG

1
Copy a prompt above → paste into Midjourney / DALL-E 3 / Stable Diffusion
2
Generate 4 variations → pick the cleanest, highest contrast one
3
If needed: open in Canva / phone editor → adjust contrast to max, convert to B&W
4
Upload to the "Upload & Trace" tab in this app
5
Adjust the threshold slider until outlines look clean and sharp
6
Download SVG → import to Blender (extrude for resin molds) or Lightburn (laser etch SS304)
7
Add to Plate Composer tab to arrange multiple designs on one plate → export full plate SVG
