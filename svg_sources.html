⚙ From SVG to Stamp Plate — The Full Workflow

1
Find & download your SVG Use the sources above to find a design. Download as .svg format. Check the license before proceeding to a commercial plate.
2
Clean up in Inkscape (free) Open in Inkscape — simplify complex paths, remove all color fills, make everything black strokes. Use Path → Simplify if the file is too complex. Flatten to a single layer.
3
Compose your plate layout Upload to the SVG Studio app — arrange multiple designs on your plate dimensions, set margins, check the engraving preview, export the final plate SVG.
4
Open in your laser software Import the plate SVG into Lightburn, EzCad2, or xTool XCS. Set your engraving parameters for SS304 mirror steel — typically 80% speed, 50% power, 500 DPI for fine detail work.
5
Engrave on SS304 mirror steel — done Run your laser engraver on the mirror finish steel plate. Clean with isopropyl alcohol after engraving. Quality control: press-test on stamp ink before packing.

💡 Pro Tips from the Field

→ The nail art stamping community uses identical designs to miniature painting stamps — search both markets when sourcing. "Nail art stamp svg" often surfaces designs not found under "stamp plate svg".
→ Goblin Hobbies (goblinhobbies.com) is the reference catalog for what sells in the miniature stamping market — study their catalog before sourcing new designs.
→ When buying on Etsy, sellers offering a "commercial license" add-on (usually +₹200 extra) are a good sign — they understand the market and the designs are likely high quality.
→ Creative Fabrica subscription at ~₹400/month pays for itself after 3–4 packs you'd have bought individually. Essential if you're producing plates regularly across multiple categories.
→ game-icons.net is the single best free source — MIT license, 4000+ stamp-perfect designs, instant SVG download, no account needed. Bookmark this before anything else.
→ Always test-trace in Inkscape before engraving — some complex SVGs have overlapping paths, hidden elements, or hairline details that won't survive engraving. Path → Object to Path, then check for issues.
→ Build a library of 20–30 tested, engrave-ready SVGs in your most popular categories before listing. Customers who order fast get repeat business — don't source on-demand for paid orders.
